History

Brief history of the Theatre

YUG Theatre was founded as a training studio by Vagif Ibrahimoglu and Hasanaga Turabov on 18th of October, 1989. The studio had participated in a number of international festivals, such as International Youth Festival in Istanbul (Turkey, 1991) and FAJR festival (Iran, 1992).

In 1992 the theatre gained state status and became the YUG State Theatre.

YUG Theatre won Grand-Prix in 1995 on the experimental theatre festival and the Best Actor Prize in 1997 on TUGANLIK International theatre festival (Ufa, Russia).

In 2003 Theatre received the restored place - architectural building of XIX century. During whole period of activity the theatre has produced more than 70 performances. Nowadays, our THEATRE has got its own theory: psychosophy, which is formed by Vagif Ibrahimoglu – Chief Director of the THEATRE.
